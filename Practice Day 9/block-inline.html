Fun Facts About US States

California

Governor: Gavin Newsom

Fun Facts

~ Largest state economy in the U.S. and one of the largest in the world.
~ Home to Silicon Valley, Hollywood, Yosemite National Park, and the Golden Gate Bridge.
~ Produces over 80% of the world's almonds.
~ California has 9 national parks, more than any other U.S. state.
