New Renewable Energy Project Launched

The $2 billion solar initiative aims to power 200,000 homes by 2027.

By Sarah Johnson

Published: June 15, 2024

Austin, TX — A new renewable energy project is set to bring thousands of jobs to Central Texas as construction begins on what will be one of the largest solar farms in the state.

The 600-megawatt facility, called the "Lone Star Solar Project," is a partnership between Austin Energy and GreenFuture Inc.
The project is expected to create over 3,500 jobs during construction and 250 permanent positions once operational.

“This project is a major step forward in our commitment to a cleaner, stronger, and more sustainable future for Texas.”

— Mayor Kirk Stone, City of Austin

Local leaders say the project will not only boost the economy but also reduce carbon emissions by an estimated 1.2 million tons annually.
